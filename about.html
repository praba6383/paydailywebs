About PayDaily

Empowering businesses worldwide with secure, reliable payment solutions since 2018

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of fintech veterans and payment industry experts, PayDaily was born from a simple vision: to make digital payments accessible, secure, and seamless for businesses of all sizes. What started as a small startup in Silicon Valley has grown into a global payment platform trusted by over 100,000 merchants worldwide.

Our journey began when our founders experienced firsthand the challenges businesses face with traditional payment processors - complex integrations, hidden fees, poor customer support, and limited global reach. They set out to build a better solution that would democratize access to world-class payment infrastructure.

Today, PayDaily processes over $50 billion in transactions annually, serving businesses from startups to Fortune 500 companies across 150+ countries. Our commitment to innovation, security, and customer success has made us one of the fastest-growing payment platforms in the industry.
